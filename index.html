Frequently Asked Questions

Who owns the copyright to the final AI-generated music?

Once full payment is completed for our premium track productions, you own 100% of the commercial distribution rights. You can upload the tracks to Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube without paying us royalties.

What do I need to submit to begin an order?

For simple song creations, you only need to send us your lyrics or describe the genre/mood you are targeting. For vocal generation projects, just provide the script file and a quick reference link of your preferred style.

What languages do you optimize your music tracks for?

We specialize primarily in Hindi, Bangla, Bhojpuri, Bhakti, and English tracks, but can structurally customize arrangements for other modern regional dialects upon custom consultation request.
