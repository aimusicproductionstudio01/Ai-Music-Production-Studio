Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At AI Music Production Studio, protecting your personal data is a priority. This Privacy Policy details how we look after your information when you stream, download, or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We receive and store information that you share directly with us, including identifiers like your name, email address, password preferences, and interaction indicators when submitting standard forms across our platform layouts.

2. Cookies

Cookies and small temporary storage indicators help preserve your local preferences (such as audio volume thresholds and explicit language configuration state). You can opt out of browser cookies directly inside your dynamic browser settings panel.

3. Analytics

We leverage automated analytics configurations to monitor system performance metrics, drop-off rates, and regional stream preferences. This metadata remains anonymized and assists purely in fine-tuning backend computational bandwidth optimization processes.

4. User Accounts

When you register a user account, your profile metadata is protected by system-level encryptions. Registered credentials allow you to save playlist links, organize preferred language folders, and synchronize dynamic streaming logs securely.

5. Newsletter Subscriptions

If you actively opt in via our email validation fields, you will receive notifications for upcoming tracks, new regional albums, and studio drops. Every informational dispatch includes a direct unsubscribe button to immediately remove your entry from active distribution rosters.

6. Download History

Our infrastructure logs specific tracks you download to satisfy local music licensing constraints and ensure accurate royalty distribution reporting to our independent distribution partners.

7. Third-Party Services

Certain dynamic features link directly through outside components, including specific social interfaces (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and YouTube APIs). These external operators execute distinct framework policies that we cannot modify directly.

8. Security

We deploy standard Transport Layer Security (TLS) methodologies to shield incoming transmission pipelines. However, please remember that no web connection layout guarantees complete absolute immunity from evolving cyber structural vulnerabilities.
